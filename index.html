Our Mission

Our mission is to work single-mindedly for the overall good of OBA, enhancing the social and economic development of the town and the progress of its people. We aim to tackle the root causes of poverty and provide essential facilities and opportunities to the underpriveledged members of our society.

Empowerment

We provide self-sufficiency by providing credit facilities, especially for the unemployed and those affected by drug abuse helping them achieve financial independence.

Learn More

Fighting Poverty

We ensure the structures of poverty in OBA by ensuring food security, increasing income, and improving health and education for the rural and underpriveledged.

Learn More

Supporting the Vunerable

We assist physically handicapped individuals, orphans, widows, and street children by financing their needs reintegrating them into society.
