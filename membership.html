About Us

IBEAYIFA was established in 1986 as an informal, non-partisan group of selected OBA citizens, wherever they may reside worldwide. Its vision is to collaborate with all stateholders in promoting the overall development in OBA. Over the years, IBEAYIFA gained an enviable reputation through the excution of different charitable and developmental projects which impacted positively on the lives of OBA people.
